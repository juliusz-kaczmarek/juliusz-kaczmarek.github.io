About me

I am a passionate Game Developer and data professional with a strong focus on data-driven solutions. My experience spans the Game Industry, Hospitality, and Retail, where I have gained valuable insights. I hold a BSc (Hons) in Computer Games Programming from Staffordshire University. Currently, I am engaged in an unannounced independent game development project while also pursuing data-driven initiatives.

I am actively seeking opportunities in data-related roles, where I can apply my technical expertise and creativity to solve complex problems.

Outside of work, I serve as a Founding Member and Vice-Chairman of the Supervisory Authority at Poznańska Gildia Graczy, an association dedicated to promoting the culture of video games in Poland.

While much of my career has revolved around gaming, my interests extend far beyond it. I am always eager to explore new fields, expand my knowledge, and embrace new challenges.
